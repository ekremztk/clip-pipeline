## Pricing FAQs

Do you provide captions and transcriptions for video clips?

Yes, we offer the option to include embedded subtitles in your video clips. Additionally, for each video clip, you can choose to download the subtitles and transcriptions separately.

What languages are supported?

Currently we support English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, Dutch, German, Italian media but we plan to add more languages soon.

What's the fastest way to reach you in case I need help?

To contact us, you can email us at [contact@flowjin.com](mailto:contact@flowjin.com), or leave a message on the live chat widget on the Flowjin website.

If I cancel my plan can I get a refund?

No. We don't have a refund policy on all subscription plans and all sales are final. Please check our [Terms of Service](https://www.flowjin.com/terms-of-service) for more details.

Do my credits carry over into the next month?

Yes! Your unused credits from the previous billing period will carry over to the next month. However, rollover credits can only carry over for one month and cannot accumulate further. This feature is available for paid plans (Starter, Creator).

Do projects expire?

Project expiration depends on your plan when you created it: Free plan projects expire after **5 days**, Starter plan expire after **60 days**, and **Creator plan projects never expire**. Once expired, you can still access and download your exported clips, but draft clips will no longer be available for editing. If you need to work on those drafts again, you'll need to re-upload and reanalyze your file, which will use credits from your account.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can easily cancel your subscription from your Flowjin account. You can also delete your account and permanently wipe your data from our servers.

What does AI Processing Hours mean?

AI Processing Hours is the amount of time our AI will process your media files. The output of processing includes assets repurposed from your media files including video clips, quote images, social copies and more.
