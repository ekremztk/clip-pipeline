## Frequently Asked Questions?

I need extra credits, how do I top-up?

The top-up feature is currently exclusive to all paid plans. To add extra credits, simply click on the credit balance on your home screen or use the "Upgrade" tab. From there, you can select your preferred plan to add more credits to your account. It's that easy!

How to upgrade my account/subscription to a Paid Plan?

To upgrade your account, go to your account settings and look for the "Upgrade" button. From there, you'll be able to choose between monthly or annual plans for either option.

Select the plan that suits your needs and follow the prompts to complete the upgrade process.

‍

How to cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, simply navigate to "Settings" in your account settings and then go to the "Billing and Plans" tab. Click on the three dots under your current plan section, and then click "Cancel."

Can I download my invoices?

Yes, under the settings tab, you can now download/view your invoices with just three clicks!
