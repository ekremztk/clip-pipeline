## Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for?
Reach out to our customer support team.

### Basics

What is Restream?

Restream is a complete live streaming solution that allows you stream from one place to 30+ social channels.

How do I get started?

Register on our website, connect platform accounts and go live in seconds right from your browser (no setup needed) or your favorite streaming tool. [Learn more](https://support.restream.io/en/articles/8540516-go-live-with-studio-2-0)

Does Restream affect my video quality?

No, Restream doesn't affect your video quality thanks to our innovative technology.

### Pricing

Can I stream for free?

Yes, you can choose 2 from 30+ social channels to multistream for free. Our paid plans offer extra channels and more robust features.

Which plan should I choose?

Our plans are based on the number of channels you need to stream to and additional features. Our support team can help you pick the plan that fits your needs.

What is custom RTMP?

Custom RTMP is a way to stream to a platform that is not listed among our [supported services](https://support.restream.io/en/articles/872029-supported-social-platforms), but provides an RTMP URL and stream key.

### Streaming

Can I stream in full HD?

With our Professional plan, you can stream in 1080p using Restream Studio. If using custom software, there's no restriction, however some platforms may not support Full HD.

Can I stream with stereo sound?

Yes, Restream Studio supports stereo sound.

Can I stream using streaming software?

Yes, you can use any 3rd party streaming software or our intuitive web-based Restream Studio. [Learn more](https://support.restream.io/en/articles/2411868-select-your-streaming-software)

### Features

Can I invite guests to the stream?

Yes, you can add guests with an invite link. Restream supports up to 10 participants. [Learn more](https://support.restream.io/en/articles/8540529-how-to-invite-guests-in-studio)

Can I stream pre-recorded videos?

Yes, you can stream pre-recorded content live with Upload & Stream. [Learn more](https://support.restream.io/en/articles/2715850-schedule-your-video-as-a-pre-recorded-event)

Can I stream to my server or custom destinations?

Yes, you can use our Custom RTMP feature and stream to any custom platform that provides an RTMP URL and stream key. [Learn more](https://support.restream.io/en/articles/369436-stream-to-a-custom-rtmp-channel)

### Resources

Do I need additional bandwidth to multistream?

No, you don't need additional bandwidth to use Restream even if you stream to 30 destinations at once.

Do you have any bitrate limits?

No, Restream doesnt’t have any bitrate limits. Note, that sometimes platforms set their bitrate limits.

Does Restream add any delay?

Any additional delay experienced during streaming is always under 2 seconds. This slight lag can occur occasionally, and the number of channels you're using won't impact it.

### Customisation

Can I switch between cameras and audio sources?

Yes, you can switch between cameras and microphones with a click.

Can I customise my stream?

Yes, Restream offers a rich collection of themes, overlays, logos, and backgrounds for any occasion. Also, you can brand your stream with your own graphics. [Learn more](https://support.restream.io/en/articles/8540835-add-graphics-to-your-stream-in-studio)

Can I stream audio only?

Yes, you can. You can start a live stream with a static image or black screen and broadcast your audio content.
